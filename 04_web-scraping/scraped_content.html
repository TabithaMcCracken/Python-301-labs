The Colorado 14ers

Colorado is home to 58 peaks above 14,000 feet of elevation, referred to as the fourteeners or 14ers. One of the most popular outdoor activities in Colorado is to climb to the summit of a 14er. If that's your goal, you've come to the right place! 14ers.com has everything you need to research and plan you 14er climbs.

* To be ranked, a peak must have at least 300 feet of prominence, which is the amount of elevation it rises above the lowest saddle that connects to the nearest, higher peak. This guideline has been in use in Colorado for some time. The following peaks are not ranked because they do not have enough prominence but are on this 14er list because they are named and recognized on USGS maps:

Mt. Cameron  Prominence 152'
El Diente Peak  Prominence 264'
Challenger Point  Prominence 264'
North Eolus  Prominence 212'
